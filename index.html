About Wealth Wizard Canada

Wealth Wizard Canada provides free Canadian-focused finance calculators to help you estimate investment growth, plan retirement savings, and understand debt payoff strategies using Canadian assumptions and CAD currency.

Our tools are built around common Canadian financial models, including RRSP contribution strategies, TFSA investment growth, retirement planning ranges, and interest calculations used in typical loans, lines of credit, and credit card debt.

Use these calculators as educational planning tools rather than precise forecasts. Real financial outcomes depend on investment performance, changing interest rates, federal or provincial policies, service fees, and inflation. If you need personalized guidance, consult a licensed financial professional.
